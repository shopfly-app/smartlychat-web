Features

  • Real-time Customer Support

    Smartly Chat offers real-time customer support for your Shopify store, keeping you closely connected with your customers. Whether it's answering questions, providing assistance, or addressing complaints, you can respond promptly and meet customer needs.

  • Intelligent Automated Replies

    With advanced AI technology, Smartly Chat provides intelligent automated replies. It learns and understands your frequently asked questions and responses, delivering accurate and quick replies automatically. This way, you can provide instant support and answers to customers even when you're offline.

  • Data Analytics and Insights

    Smartly Chat offers powerful data analytics tools, giving you deep insights into customer interactions and behavior. By analyzing chat records and metrics, you gain valuable insights into customer preferences, popular topics, and areas for service improvement. This helps you optimize your marketing strategies and enhance customer satisfaction.
